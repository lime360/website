What is Hoverboy?

Hoverboy - is a platformer game that takes inspiration from Sonic the Hedgehog series.

The story involves a teenage guy who is trying to stop Dr. Physic, an evil genius that is going to make money by destroying The Earth.

THIS GAME IS A HUGE WORK IN PROGRESS, EVERYTHING THAT YOU SEE IN DEMOS ARE A SUBJECT TO CHANGE

Controls & System Requirements

  • Windows XP or never (PC version)
  • Android 4.4 or never (Mobile version)
  • Any HTML5 supported browser (HTML5 Demo + April Fools Demo)
